How the PlayStation 1 Changed Gaming

PlayStation 1 revolutionized the world of gaming with its powerful hardware, immersive 3D graphics, and vast library of diverse titles. It brought gaming to a wider audience and set new standards for entertainment, making it one of the most influential consoles in history.

20 Most Played PS1 Games in Japan

  • バイオハザード (Biohazard) - Resident Evil

    Biohazard / Resident Evil

    Release Date: March 22, 1996

    Overview: A survival horror game where players navigate a mansion overrun by zombies and creatures, uncovering a conspiracy involving bioweapons.

  • ファイナルファンタジーVII (Fainaru Fantajī VII) - Final Fantasy VII

    Final Fantasy VII

    Release Date: January 31, 1997

    Overview: An epic RPG following Cloud Strife and Avalanche as they battle against the corrupt Shinra corporation and Sephiroth, exploring themes of identity and environmentalism.

  • メタルギアソリッド (Metaru Gia Soriddo) - Metal Gear Solid

    Metal Gear Solid

    Release Date: September 3, 1998

    Overview: Stealth action game where Solid Snake infiltrates a nuclear weapons disposal facility to stop terrorists from launching a nuclear strike.

About Luís Otávio Dias de Oliveira

Hello! My name is Luís Otávio Dias de Oliveira, and I am passionate about video games, particularly retro games from the 90s and early 2000s. Ever since I was a child, I have been fascinated by the evolution of gaming technology and storytelling, which has led me to develop a deep appreciation for classic consoles like the PlayStation 1 and PSP.

Growing up in Brazil, I spent countless hours playing, exploring, and analyzing games that shaped my childhood and influenced my aspirations. My favorite genres include RPGs, adventure games, and fighting games, all of which have contributed to my understanding of game design and narrative development.

Throughout my journey, I have also developed an interest in game preservation and the history of gaming. I believe that understanding the roots of modern gaming helps us appreciate the artistry and innovation behind each title. This passion has motivated me to create this website, which serves both as a personal project and as a tribute to the games and consoles I love.

Apart from gaming, I enjoy programming, exploring new technologies, and learning about digital art and design. I am always eager to expand my skills and collaborate with others who share similar interests.

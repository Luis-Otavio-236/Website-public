How the PlayStation 1 Changed Gaming

PlayStation 1 revolutionized the world of gaming with its powerful hardware, immersive 3D graphics, and vast library of diverse titles. It brought gaming to a wider audience and set new standards for entertainment, making it one of the most influential consoles in history.

20 Most Played PS1 Games in Japan

  • バイオハザード (Biohazard) - Resident Evil

    Biohazard / Resident Evil

    Release Date: March 22, 1996

    Overview: A survival horror game where players navigate a mansion overrun by zombies and creatures, uncovering a conspiracy involving bioweapons.

  • ファイナルファンタジーVII (Fainaru Fantajī VII) - Final Fantasy VII

    Final Fantasy VII

    Release Date: January 31, 1997

    Overview: An epic RPG following Cloud Strife and Avalanche as they battle against the corrupt Shinra corporation and Sephiroth, exploring themes of identity and environmentalism.

  • メタルギアソリッド (Metaru Gia Soriddo) - Metal Gear Solid

    Metal Gear Solid

    Release Date: September 3, 1998

    Overview: Stealth action game where Solid Snake infiltrates a nuclear weapons disposal facility to stop terrorists from launching a nuclear strike.

  • ドラゴンクエストVII (Doragon Kuesuto VII) - Dragon Quest VII

    Dragon Quest VII

    Release Date: September 17, 2000

    Overview: An expansive role-playing game where players explore a vast world, uncover secrets, and battle monsters in a classic turn-based system.

  • グランツーリスモ (Guran Tsūrisumo) - Gran Turismo

    Gran Turismo

    Release Date: December 23, 1997

    Overview: A realistic racing simulation featuring a wide variety of cars and tracks, emphasizing driving physics and customization.

  • テイルズ オブ ファンタジア (Teiruzu Obu Fantajia) - Tales of Phantasia

    Tales of Phantasia

    Release Date: December 15, 1995

    Overview: An action RPG with real-time battles, following a group of heroes on a quest to save the world from chaos.
